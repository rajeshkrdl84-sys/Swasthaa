    The Three Forces

    Tri-Guna

    Beyond the body — the three qualities of mind and consciousness that determine how healing happens

    ☀
    Sattva
    Purity · Clarity · Light

    The healing state. When Sattva dominates — the body repairs, the mind clarifies, and true health emerges.

    🔥
    Rajas
    Activity · Passion · Motion

    The driving force. Rajas initiates change — but without Sattva, becomes the source of disease.

    🌑
    Tamas
    Inertia · Darkness · Rest

    The resting state. Necessary for recovery — but in excess, the root of stagnation and chronic disease.
